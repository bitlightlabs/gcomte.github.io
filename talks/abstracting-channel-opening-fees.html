Abstracting channel opening fees

  • Not charging the user channel opening fees
  • Instead Lipa (or the LSP?) pays
  • User reimburses through alternative means
    • Routing fees
    • Subscription
    • Ads
    • Miner within Lipa Wallet
    • ...

Incentives

How can we incentivize users to open channels when fees are low?

  • Remember fixed 2.5k sat cost
  • Fewer incentives = more cost
  • Gamification?
  • Timely advertisement?
  • Playing with fee structure?

Incentives

  • User needs to pay cost
  • More expensive = worse UX?

Lipa Budgeting

Rant against subsidizing

What if Lipa just subsidizes users until we have a big user base and then ... *magic*

  • Never subsidize in a startup *
  • Product/Service must forward cost to consumer
  • Cost is not about income
  • Cost assesses customer value and determines its scalability

Alternative measures

  • Smarter channel sizes
  • Additional incentives
  • Communicate value proposition

Smarter channel sizes

  • Actually minimize channel opening cost
  • Find out best fitting channel size per user
  • E.g. stacker vs tech enthusiast
  • Complex endeavour

Additional incentives

  • Inform people when inbound capacity is low
  • Animate them to create inbound capacity
  • Inform people about on-chain fee market
  • Animate them to open channels when fees are low
  • When exactly are fees low?
