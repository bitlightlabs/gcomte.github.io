Privacy on L1

Blockchain is public!

  • L1 is pseudonymous, not anonymous
  • Linking of addresses to identities (KYC)
  • Tracking movement of identified coins
  • IP tracking (less relevant)

Improve your L1 privacy

  • Limit linkage of your identity to your coins
  • Try obfuscating already identified coins
  • Don't link 'anonymous' coins with already identified coins
  • DON'T REUSE ADDRESSES
  • Don't leak your IP

Privacy on LN

  • Payments are (pretty) private
  • Your identity (public key) never changes
  • Announced channels are tied to your public key
  • Probing is real
  • Unannounced channels are (pretty) private
